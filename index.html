Welcome to Augmented Universe System (AUS)

AUS is a is an entirely new type of gaming. Imagine the Wii and the Gameboy combined. When you move in the real world, you move in AUS. That's right its one step closer to the Virtually Reality you have always dreamed of. And its all in the palm of your hand. Capable of being played on any modern smart phone or tablet with Internet connection.

Technology and Tools Used

Do buzz words or trendy frameworks give you goose bumps? Well AUS is built with the latest web technology. The backend is wired up with Nodejs and Express, the front end is presented with Angular, the two chat with each other with SockJS and the data is held together by MongoDB, that's one mean stack ;-)
